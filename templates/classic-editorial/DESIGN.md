# Classic Editorial

A quiet, magazine-style brand. The same serif (Newsreader) carries both
headline and body; the lone accent (`primary`) appears only on the title
rule and call-to-action buttons. Best for essays, deep dives, and weekly
reads where the writing is the product.

The runtime mirror of these tokens is the `classic-editorial` entry in
`src/shared/templates.ts`. Edit either the tokens here or in the studio's
design panel — both express the same DESIGN.md shape.
